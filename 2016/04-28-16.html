Kickstarting Modernization Through the Cloud

Part of what makes the EMC {code} team successful is the connections to both individuals in the EMC {code} Community and partners. One such partner that we are excited to have the opportunity to feature is Cloud 66. This week the CEO and Co-Founder of Cloud 66, Khash Sajadi took to our blog to speak about his excitement around EMC World and the innovation inside tech.

Sajadi writes that "in order to remain relevant, enterprises are left with no other choice than to adapt their tried and tested methods to herald in the new." He continues to address the importance of being agile and flexible while also recognizing the need for "a seismic cultural shift." Visit the blog for more!
