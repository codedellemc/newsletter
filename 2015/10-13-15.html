EMC Helps Bring Persistent Storage Access to Apache Mesos

Mesosphere and  EMC's {code} team teamed up to address persistent storage with Mesos. On Thursday last week , we announced  (press release here)  that  the Mesosphere Datacenter Operating System (DCOS) can now support any block-based storage system that currently has a working Docker plugin. It's a list that includes  EMC ScaleIO ,  Amazon EBS ,  OpenStack Cinder  and more.

This is delivered by two projects-- you can learn more about specific functions here:
  • mesos module dvdi
  • dvdcli
This is big news for both Mesosphere customers, who can now support persistent data use cases, and EMC customers, who are looking to integrate Mesos DCOS into their EMC environment. For more details:
  • Technical Blog Post on the EMC {code} Blog
  • Video demonstration
  • EMC Pulse Post
  • Mesos Blog Post
