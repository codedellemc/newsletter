Introducing RackHD CLI

The team is continuing our efforts in enabling the open source tool RackHD for bare-metal integrations, which led us to the creation of RackHD CLI (command line interface)! After all,  CLIs are critical to system administrators who want to gain quick insights into an application and enable more powerful behavior through scripting. By using gorackHD golang bindings, we were able to easily create this latest project and develop a baseline for continued functionality.
