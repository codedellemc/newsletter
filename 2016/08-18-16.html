The Hot Aisle Podcast 
 
The Hot Aisle Podcast co-hosted by Brian Carpenter and Brent Piatti, is focused on delivering content for the next generation of Systems Engineers (SEs) while giving customers and partners equal access to the journey. The podcast has unique origins as well. While Brent and Brian both had similar jobs and knew of each other, neither had really interacted with the other. 

Late in 2015 Brent noticed occasional gaps in learning when it came to a variety of tools and opportunities for SEs. One of the solutions he brought to his management was the opportunity to host a podcast that helped fill in these gaps. At the same time, Brian had been discussing a light-hearted podcast with peer Tommy Trogden. The focus would be to read Chad Sakac's notoriously verbose blog posts and emails, and add commentary - calling it tl;dr cast. As Brian mentioned the idea to a leadership member who had just received Brent's podcast proposal - let's just say the two were destined to meet. A few days and a few twisted arms later, The Hot Aisle was born. The rest, as they say, is History!

The Hot Aisle has been graced by great guests from all over the industry, but of course we had to put them on the spot for a short "must listen to" list of episodes:
  • Episode 45: Rancher
  • Episode 44: Kubernetes
  • Episode 39: Morpheus
  • Episode 30: Mesosphere
  • Episode 16: Open Source Community

Turning 25 in Style

On August 22nd, Linux users, developers and container enthusiasts descend on Toronto's Westin Harbour Castle for LinuxCon and ContainerCon North America (NA). The team is looking forward to our week at ContainerCon NA. Here's a few highlights of what you can expect at the co-located event from the EMC {code} team. 
  • Kendrick Coleman, Developer Advocate for EMC {code} is speaking on August 24th (Wed.) at 11:55AM on Highly Available and Distributed Containers. Don't miss out!
  • The EMC {code} Team can be found at booth #44 in the Expo Hall! Stop by and learn more about the {code} community and our available open source projects. 
  • Pancake Robot from The New Stack will be flipping pancakes and hosting the podcast live on August 24th to talk about the open source ecosystem and the state of containers. 
  • Thursday, August 25th is the co-located Open Source Storage Summit from 9-4PM. The event is free to attend and we are proud to announce that Mike Coleman (Docker) and Chris Duchesne (EMC {code}) will be speaking at the event.
