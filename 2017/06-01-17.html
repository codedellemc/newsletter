{code} Org.

This week Jonas Rosland attended the Open Source Summit in Japan, presenting his session on "Leading an Open Source Community at a large Enterprise" to a packed room. The audience had questions on how to create a community of their own, get buy-in from their management and how we handle breaches of our Code of Conduct. You can view his presentation here.


Jonas also visited the Dell EMC Japan office to talk about open source at Dell EMC and the work the {code} team has done around container technologies, with a focus on REX-Ray and container storage implementations for Docker, Mesos and Kubernetes. A lot of great questions came from the team, which led to deep discussions on how to manage containers in a modern datacenter and how open source has changed the infrastructure landscape for both our customers and us.
