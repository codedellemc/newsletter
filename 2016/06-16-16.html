Four Days to DockerCon

We are stoked to see some of our partners at DockerCon this year and many of whom joined us at EMC World! You may even recognize a few of the names: Avi Networks, Docker (obviously), Mesosphere, Rancher and so many more. Come join us on the Expo Hall floor and  don't forget to stop by these three sessions that are sure to be hot topics throughout the halls of DockerCon: 
  • Monday: Joshua Bernstein (VP of Technology, EMC) will be speaking on Monday, June 20 at 2:55PM in Rooms 618-620 on "Data Persistence in the New Container World by EMC."
  • Tuesday: Jonas Rosland (Developer Advocate) will be speaking at 11:15AM in Room 609 on "Open Source is Good for both Business and Humanity."
  • Tuesday: Kenny Coleman (Developer Advocate) will be speaking at 1:30PM in Rooms 618-620 on "Highly Available Persistent Applications in Containers."
