{code} Community

The {code} Community reached a huge milestone last week! We now have over 2,000 open source focused members! With over 330,000 messages and 4,700 files shared over the past year and a half, our dedicated members continue to share projects, war stories, funny anecdotes and of course programming support.

When we started the {code} Community Slack team a little over a year ago we had no idea we would reach so many smart and innovative people this quickly. We are humbled and thankful for each person who participates in collaborating on projects, sharing knowledge, being supportive and of course simply being amazing members of the open source community.

We look forward to continue working with and growing the {code} Community with more projects and members. 

You all rock!

{code} Org.

This week the {code} team was over at KubeCon and during the first keynote by Dan Kohn, the Executive Director of Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) shared a slide focused on the Cloud Native Landscape (v0.9). Among others, {code} and specifically REX-Ray, was pictured as a Cloud Native Storage solution. Thanks Dan for the call out! 

For us, this really speaks to the focus of {code} -  storage solutions for container platforms, including Kubernetes. The team has a proven track record of making successful storage technologies available to modern and open source infrastructure. Take these solutions on a test run through our new {code} Labs repo! 

For more on these storage solutions visit our blog.
