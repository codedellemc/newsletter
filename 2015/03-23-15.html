March Madness: It's Conference Season!

CloudFoundry Dojo Launches in EMC Cambridge!

Things are heating up in Boston (and it's about time after 7' of snow), and the EMC Kendall Square office (where EMC {code} is anchored) is quickly becoming a hub for Boston-based developers and DevOps professionals.

 

CloudFoundry and EMC are bringing a Dojo to EMC Cambridge this summer, with the goal of training over 150 developers. The concept of the Cloud Foundry Dojo is about rapid learning and development of applications through "pair programming" that puts developers shoulder-to-shoulder with Cloud Foundry gurus for 6-12 week open source projects designed to breed qualified Cloud Foundry engineers with "committer" status. This concept began in the coding Dojo of Pivotal Labs, which EMC acquired in 2012-and is now part of Pivotal Software.

Interested in contributing to OpenSource but not sure where to start? It might seem daunting, but sometimes a contribution can be as simple as a typo or bug fix, or adding to documentation. These two resources are great for finding a project to get your feet wet with OpenSource-and to work on something outside of the realm of your day-to-day.

 

  • CodeMontage: Contribute to projects that help social causes, such as civic engagement, disaster relief, and international development.
