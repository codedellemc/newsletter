code.community
DockerCon Europe 2015

DockerCon is wrapping up in Barcelona, and there were a lot of interesting announcements and developments in the Docker Community.

EMC {code} attended to learn, network, and showcase some of the Docker-related projects we've been working on ( REX-Ray, Docker Volume Driver interfaces for Mesos & more).

We were excited to see that Docker is solidifying their platform strategy,  with Swarm and Engine merging. Read more on Docker's Blog and the video footage on the DockerCon web site.

code.team
Free Docker Workshop with Swarm, Storage, and Networking

EMC {code} was a sponsor at WebSummit 2015 and gave a 3 hour Docker 1.9 Workshop. After a very successful test run, we are able to allow anyone to use the workshop at their very own convenience!

In the self-paced workshop, you'll learn:
  • Docker basics, including docker run, Dockerfiles and more
  • Docker 1.9 enhancements with storage and networking
  • Data persistence with Docker
  • and more!

Read more about the training on our blog!


 

Of course, we'd love to know how it goes for you, so feel free to drop us a line on Twitter or join our CodeCommunity, where we have a channel dedicated to Docker training.
