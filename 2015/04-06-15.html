First Geek Week, In the Books!

 

 

 

Matt Cowger (@mcowger) and Jonas Rosland (@jonasrosland) taught the first of many DevOps/Agile GeekWeeks last week to a pilot group of vSpecialists. 

 

During the class, they learned how to use Kanban boards for project management, do daily stand ups, use modern tools for communication and collaboration such as Slack and Google Hangout, master Git and GitHub, use the key-value DB Redis, store objects in ViPR S3. Participants then had to combine all this knowledge to create a 12-factor application that's deployed live on CloudFoundry. 

 

The course will be rolled out to all vSpecialists globally in the coming months, and if you're interested in looking at the course content, it's public and available on GitHub!
