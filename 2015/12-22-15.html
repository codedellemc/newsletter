A Community with Traditions

'Tis the season for traditions both new and old. Stephanie Carlson ( from the EMC {code} Team) wanted to know what traditions the {code} Community has and asked "What is their holiday tradition?" 

Jonas Rosland  said that "he celebrates both Swedish and American Christmas (on different days) with their respective traditions (did someone say pickled herring)." 

For Damian Karlson ({code} Community Member) it's "enjoying  Star Wars, Elf and  Polar Express, and also grabbing a mug of hot chocolate while decorating the tree or putting up some lights."

Traditions bring culture and value to the Community. Do you have traditions you'd like to share? Join the {code} Community on Slack or send us a tweet @EMCcode to be a part of the conversation.

EMC {code} Meets with EMC Leadership

Last week the EMC {code} team spent time together as a team.  Thursday was our team bonding day where we spent time connecting and getting to know one another on a more personal level, since we're spread out across the US and don't see each other face to face every day. We were able to share lots of interesting stories, anecdotes and hilarious commentaries together. 

On Friday, the team had an amazing opportunity to meet with Jeremy Burton (President of Products and Marketing at EMC) and CJ Desai (President of Emerging Technology Division at EMC), asking us about what we see as the future of open source at EMC and how vital EMC {code} is to continuing this initiative. The EMC {code} Team is honored and grateful for this opportunity and we hope to continue to see added value created both internally and externally in 2016.

We also spent time a recapping of what each team member has been up to this quarter and discussed the EMC {code} 2016 Roadmap. 

We are excited for what is to come in the New Year.
