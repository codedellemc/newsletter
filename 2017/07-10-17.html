{code} Community

The {code} team is in Denver this week participating in GopherCon. One of the highlights is that our very own {code} engineer Vladimir Vivien is here giving away signed copies of his book to the Go community! After programming in Go for a couple of years, Vladimir published a book on the subject titled "Learning Go Programming" an insightful guide to learning the Go programming language. Vladimir wrote the book because he wanted to make it easier for newcomers to get into the language - essentially writing the book he would have liked to have when he was starting out with Go.

A huge thank you to everyone that came up and talked to us at GopherCon, and enjoy your books!



{code} Org.

The first community meeting for the Container Storage Interface (CSI) project occurred on Wednesday, July 12th. The group is making progress with activities such as the specification going through a final round of tweaks, implementations, and new designs. The bi-weekly meeting is open for everyone to discuss open issues and learn about the updates to the CSI Specification. See a recording of the this past meeting at the following google group link (mtg starts about the 5 min mark).
 
To join future calls, refer to the call details in the folder CSI regular community sync.
