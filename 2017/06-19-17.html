80 enrolled for {code} Catalyst Mike Coleman's  @Docker Swarm workshop held on 15-June and {code} team member Steve Wong attended. Docker Evangelist Mike Coleman presented a 90 minute hands-on workshop where attendees deployed personal Swarm clusters on the Azure public cloud. If you missed it, the step by step lesson plan is here: https://github.com/mikegcoleman/hybrid-workshop .
 
CNCF Kubernetes Fundamentals
Earlier this year, the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) unveiled a self-paced course entitled Kubernetes Fundamentals (LFS258). The course is a fantastic resource for anyone, end-users or developers, looking to get an introduction into the world of Kubernetes. The content and hands-on labs cover the building blocks for understanding key concepts and how to manage a Kubernetes cluster. Additionally, the course highlights and briefly touch upon some advanced topics that happening in the space, such as monitoring, third-party resources, and Helm just to name a few. This training comes highly recommend for anyone starting out or is interesting learning more about Kubernetes.
