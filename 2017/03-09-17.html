Toward a new contract with infrastructure

Businesses thrive on predictability and so does software. Applications built in the last 20 years rely heavily on their infrastructure to help it function. But as many of us have seen, that's changed. To move forward, we need what may be thought of as "a contract with cloud native services:" a set of industry agreements and interfaces that ensure interoperability.
