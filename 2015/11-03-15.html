code.community
ContainerDays NYC! 

We were happy to be a part of ContainerDays NYC last week and reconnect with some great local community members, EMC'ers and partners. 

ContainerDays is a small, community-organized conference focused on-- you guessed it-- container technology. Sessions ranged from an ecosystem view of containers, to hands-on technical sessions.

This year, EMC {code} was a silver sponsor, and  @jonasrosland delivered a session on the Docker Toolbox. All of the conference content is available on YouTube, and you can always find EMC {code} training and labs open and available on our GitHub page.

Thanks to everyone we met at ContainerDays NYC and to the great team of volunteers and organizers!
