{code} Community

Our own Jonas Rosland at {code} not only manages the {code} Community, but is also a co-organizer for the Docker Boston Meetup. 

Recently, he was part of Docker's 4th birthday party which was celebrated in over 130 different locations across the globe.  By helping Docker enthusiasts and newcomers alike learn from experienced users, companies and organizations, the Docker Meetup is a great place to start and stay with the Docker Community.
 
Read more on Jonas' blog about what drove him to join the community and what he enjoys most out of it (not just cake).

REX-Ray has a new home! And no, it's not a dog house.
