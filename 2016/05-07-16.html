HELLO WORLD
EMC World has come to a close and we are beginning to turn our attention to OSCON 2016. But before we do, let's take a little time to reflect on EMC World. This 4-day conference is not something we can unpack in a day, but here's a few of our favorite highlights. 

We'll continue to distill, aggregate and share news related to the EMC {code} Community and industry happenings. We'll provide the content, but we need your feedback. You can always reach us at  emccode@emc.com .
code.community
Community Partners Join Us at EMC World

Over the past week, some of our partners joined us at EMC World. Each of these partners were able to spend time in our booth meeting the community surrounding EMC {code} and sharing what it is they are passionate about.  We provided a theater in our booth for our partners to provide in depth sessions on what is happening in the industry. Over 300 people attended these 25 minute sessions. To say the sessions were a big hit is an understatement since the audience frequently spilled into the walkway.  In addition we featured several guest blogs on the EMC {code} blog site -  Avi Networks ,  Cloud 66 , Mesosphere and Rancher ( Part 1 & Part 2 ).

 

Thank you to all of our partners ( Avi Networks, Cloud 66, Docker, GitHub, Mesosphere, Puppet, RackHD and Rancher) who were able to join us this week at EMC World. 
code.team
EMC World Comes to a Close

This past week was simply incredible. Here's some favorite pictures and highlights from the event.
  • We announced the new initiative Polly on May 4th
  • Joshua Bernstein, VP of Technology for ETD was featured on both EMC TV and theCube this week as well as saved 007 (Jeremy Burton, President of Products and Marketing) from a couple of scrapes
  • We met many of you (our readers and community members) face to face for the first time
  • There was tons of excitement amongst the conference attendees on open source and containers
  • There were over 300 attendees who sat through the Partner Presentations in our booth
  • We exceeded capacity in not one but three of our 16 Code & Modern Operations talks and repeated one session
  • The vLab "Docker, Mesos and ScaleIO for your Persistent Applications" was one of the top five most utilized hands-on-lab 
These are just a small sampling of what we enjoyed this week and we can't wait to share more. 

Thank you to all who made our time at EMC World such a huge success.
