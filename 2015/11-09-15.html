Mars Challenge at LISA! 

This week, EMC {code} is at LISA'15, the USENIX conference for system adminstrators. We brought the EMC {code} Mars Challenge to DC, and kicked off this evening with four motivated teams. As I write this (at 11.30 pm) three of the teams are still hacking away to take away the grand prize tomorrow night.

The Mars Challenge debuted at LinuxCon in Seattle, and challenges participants to use modern technology and practices like containers, distributed databases, continuous delivery, and distributed application deployment-- to create a shield that protects them from asteroids and heat flares. The competition is high! 

Stay tuned to the newsletter or follow us on Twitter to see who took home the prize!
