Everything EMC at Web Summit

Web Summit kicks off next week in Dublin, and EMC & Pivotal have a couple of great activities happening: 

Docker & EMC {code} Tutorial, 11/4 at 1pm, in the Workshops Area.
EMC {code} is teaming up with Docker to deliver a tutorial session. The event is free for all Web Summit attendees and will cover how to run your first Docker container, networking and deploying applications with Docker. We'll also get into some more advanced data persistence use cases with EMC {code}'s REX-Ray. Every attendee will receive a Dublin Pass, which gives you access to the Guiness/Jameson factories, and many more historical sites!   EMC {code} and Docker Tutorial Registration

IOT Roundtable, 11/4 at 10:35am If you  are interested in  IoT  you will not  want to  miss  EMC's  Ed Walsh , Dr. Said  Tabet  and GE's  Dr. Joe Salvo  on the Machine Summit stage .  Come  hear  how top companies  like GE  and their customers  are gaining value from successful  Internet of Things  implementations . The panelists  will discuss the new collaborations these projects require including one of the most successful,  Project Infinite  with the Cork Institute of Technology.
