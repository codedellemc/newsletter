{code} Community

On Monday, February 20th  Stephanie Carlson from {code} joined the Women in Engineering (WIE) group from Dell EMC Isilon to host a Kids Coding Day at the office. One aspect of WIE is to bring technical education to the community and they did just that. Over  30 students ranging in  ages from 5-13 showed up, ready and eager to learn. 

The day began with some hands-on activities including a tour  of the server lab (don't worry, they didn't bring down any servers) and pulling apart a couple of computers to learn more about what makes them tick. 

Following these activities we spent time on their  choice of coding experiences. Across the room you could see kids in Scratch , Code Monkey and Hour of Code . Everyone worked through the exercises and raised their hands to get help and answers from the many volunteers.

The last activity was a live coding session where students learned how to write HTML and CSS. They saw their changes instantly on an actual website that they built.  Rebecca Tolmach led the room step by step through building a website. Each kiddo was able to customize it to match his or her style. Their excitement when the page turned blue or had dotted lines could be heard throughout the entire room. 

What an incredible outreach program! And really, what an awesome way to spend a day in the office!
