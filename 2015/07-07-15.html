HELLO WORLD

A few weeks ago, our team spent some time in the Cloudscaling offices in San Francisco to plan for the second half of the year. It might be the lazy, hazy days of summer, but {code} is moving faster than ever. We have some great projects, events, and plans coming up for the community over the next months. Check out our events calendar to see where we'll be during the back half of 2015, and make sure to keep your eye on the newsletter for info on new EMC {code} projects.

 

We hope you'll enjoy this newsletter. We'll do our best to distill, aggregate and share news related to the {code} community and industry happenings.We'll provide the content, but we need your feedback. You can always reach us at emccode-newsletter@emc.com

EMC {code} is a sponsor at LinuxCon, ContainerCon and CloudOpen in Seattle

 

 

The week of August 17th-21st brings a quadfecta of great OSS conferences to the Seattle area. Mark your calendars: EMC {code} will be an official sponsor of the simultaneous LinuxCon, ContainerCon and CloudOpen. MesosCon kicks off during the second half of the week, too.


 

While we're there, we'll be sharing some of the projects we're working on and spending time meeting community members.

Go, Go, Go! 


This week, in beautiful Denver, CO, a handful of EMC {code} team members are attending #GopherCon, an event focused on the Go programming language. Go, created by Google, has exploded in popularity and is used in production by some big names like Docker, Dropbox, SoundCloud, SendGrid and more.

 

We'll be attending sessions all week and participating in the Friday hackathon-- come say hi if you're at GopherCon this week.
