Photon Platform Devbox from VMware

Our good friends over at VMware are hard at work on the Photon Platform, a container management platform, and has just released a new way to try it out: the Photon Platform Devbox. It's a setup of two virtual machines that will let you get familiar with the concept of the Photon Machine and Photon Controller. Read more and find out all about it on the VMware blog. 
code.team
New Enhancements to the Mesos External Volume Module

We are trilled to announce the release of mesos-module-dvdi 0.4.0. Mesos (for those of you that don't know) is an enormously popular cluster management and application deployment platform. It powers the data centers of Airbnb, Twitter, Uber and many other well known internet and mobile services. This most recent valuable update includes pre-emptive mount management, isolation of external mounts and a few others. For more information on these new enhancements checkout the latest blog from our very own Steve Wong.
