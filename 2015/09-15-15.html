The CodeCommunity is growing

In June we started an experiment to see if there was enough interest to keep in touch with the {code} team and the rest of the community, so we opened a Slack team called CodeCommunity. We quickly ramped up a number of members, and now we're over 150 members strong! Do you want to be a part of it, talk about automation, infrastructure as code, argue over why Go is better than Python and more? Join us today!



code.team
REX-Ray 0.2 released

REX-Ray is quickly becoming much more stable, working on more platforms and getting more functionality. The team and the community around the project is striving toward making REX-Ray a responsible OSS projects with several things in place such as release tags, RPM/DEB/binary distribution, configuration through standard means, while also enhancing functionality and incorporating larger changes. If you want to see what's new, head over to the REX-Ray  wiki and read all about it.
