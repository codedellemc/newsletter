{code} Community

Last week the {code} team was in Denver for GopherCon and it was a blast! We spent time listening to great speakers, talking to amazing Go community members, giving away signed copies of   Vladimir Vivien's " Learning Go Programming " book and last but definitely not least, learning new things in Go from leading experts in the field.
 
David vonThenen went to a great workshop, led by Bill Kennedy, called the "Ultimate Go Workshop". Here is what he has to say about it:
"I took the Ultimate Go Workshop at GopherCon, and I thought this was an awesome class. It explained Go design considerations as it relates to optimizing to the underlying hardware (mainly optimizing for L1 Cache hits), performance as it relates to garbage collection (how it gets scheduled) and your application, memory consumption and profiling of your app, and how to debug in order to fix them."
 
We encourage you to take a look at the Go training.
